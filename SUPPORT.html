Frequently Asked Questions

Account Management

How do I create an account?

  1. Download the SCBC App
  2. Tap "Sign Up" on the welcome screen
  3. Enter your email and create a password
  4. Follow the verification steps

How do I reset my password?

  1. Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen
  2. Enter your email address
  3. Follow the reset instructions sent to your email

Events

How do I join an event?

  1. Browse events in the "Events" tab
  2. Tap on an event you're interested in
  3. Click "Join Event" to RSVP

How do I create an event?

  1. Navigate to the "Events" tab
  2. Tap the "+" button
  3. Fill in event details
  4. Submit for approval

Notifications

How do I manage notifications?

  1. Go to your device settings
  2. Find SCBC App in the notifications section
  3. Customize your notification preferences

What types of notifications will I receive?

  • Event reminders
  • New book announcements
  • Comment responses
  • Important updates

Technical Support

The app is running slowly or crashing

Try these steps:
  1. Close and reopen the app
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Update to the latest version
  4. If problems persist, contact support

Bug Reports

If you encounter a bug, please include the following information:

  1. What you were doing when the issue occurred
  2. A screenshot if possible
  3. Your device type and OS version
  4. Email the details to seattlechinatownbookclub@gmail.com with subject "Bug Report"

App Permissions

The SCBC App requires these permissions:

  • Camera (for profile photos)
  • Photo Library (for uploading images)
  • Notifications (for event reminders)
  • Location (city-level, for event organization)

Feature Requests
